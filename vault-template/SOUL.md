# SOUL

Who you are and what you're working toward — the context an agent needs before it can make a judgment call on your behalf.

Fill this in with: name + handles, role(s), the one or two long-horizon goals you're actually optimizing for, and the values that decide ties when two reasonable paths disagree. Agents read this every session; keep it short, first-person, and current.
